Possessions is coming exclusively on Apple Arcade!

Hey there! Possessions has been in works for quite a while now (three years actually) but nothing else could have made the timing of this announcement better.

After keeping it under the wraps for a year, we are finally proud to share that Possessions will be coming soon to Apple devices via their new and anticipated games subscription service called Apple Arcade.

It’s a $5/month subscription where you get up to 100 games for free, without any of those pesky ads or forced monetization. And, Possessions is glad to be one of those cool-looking 100 games.

When can you play the game? Good question! Expect to play Possessions on an Apple device you (very) very soon!

Meanwhile, have you checked out the official website of the game? You can even sign-up for updates if you are into those things. We don’t send spams, just important updates like this one.

Psst! Did you spot the game’s icon on stage during Apple’s Special Event which happened 10th September 2019?
