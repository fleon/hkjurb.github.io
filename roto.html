Immerse yourself in this circular adventure filled with big and mean balls. ROTO is a fresh take on the puzzle-platformer genre with its ultra-simple gameplay accompanied by a neat and minimal greyscale art style that will test your reflexes and will make you stick to it.


Publisher: Weraku Games


A nominee in ‘Best F2P Game’ award at Casual Connect, global App Store and Play Store homepage features.
